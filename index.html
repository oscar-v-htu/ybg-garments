FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How long does tailoring take? +
For standard orders, it takes 5-7 working days. Express service is available for urgent events.
Do you deliver to Ho Technical University? +
Yes! We offer nationwide delivery via VIP parcel service or direct courier to HTU campus.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept MTN Mobile Money (MoMo), Telecel Cash, and Bank Transfers.
Can I provide my own fabric? +
Absolutely. While we provide premium fabrics, you are welcome to bring your own material for a custom "labor-only" charge.
